Beef Bowl

Ingredients:

- Ground Beef

- Rice

- Greek Yogurt

- Red Pepper Sauce

- Cinnamon

- Onions and Tomatoes

Recipe:

1. Cook ground beef
2. Add greek yogurt to bowl
3. Mix beef, greek yogurt, and red pepper sauce together
5. Heat mixture in microwave for 30 seconds
4. Put rice in another bowl
5. Top rice with beef mixture
6. Sprinkle cinnamon over mixture
7. Top with onions and tomatoes
